[Game Master]: # 🏴‍☢️ Escape Room Scenario: "The Ironclad's Last Hunt"

## 1. The Narrative (Intro & Atmosphere)

**Setting:** *Deep Sea, Year 2045* – A forgotten fortress known as the "Ironclad." It stands in the middle of a stormy ocean, surrounded by jagged rocks and shifting tides that have once erased all records from its history. The air is thick with salt spray and the faint, metallic scent of ozone and old wood.

**The Setup:**
You are **Captain Elias Thorne**, a captain who has been leading the expedition since 1983. You are now in the main room where you have discovered the location of the "Lost Ship." Inside lies the *Ironclad*, a massive wooden vessel that was never found again.

**The Atmosphere:**
You are surrounded by **Silent Silos** – ancient, rusted structures containing preserved artifacts from the past. The walls flicker with static electricity as you walk through them. You hear the rhythmic *thump-thump-thump* of a massive clockwork mechanism in the corner. It sounds like a heartbeat that has been beating for centuries. Your first clue is hidden beneath a piece of old leather in Silo A.

---

## 2. Main Objective

**Objective:** Navigate through the three rooms to find the "Lost Ship" (The *Ironclad*) and collect all five items, then return to Captain Thorne safely.
**Constraint:** You must answer three riddles correctly to unlock the final room. Each correct answer gives you a hint, which is given as an item when you find it.

## 4. Items & Puzzles

### **Items (Total: 5)**
1.  **Rusty Key** (Room A): *A small, rusty wooden cylinder with a notched edge. It fits into the lock of Silo A's door.*
2.  **Silver Key** (Room B): *A smooth, silver metal ring that fits into the lock of Silo B's door.*
3.  **Silver Key** (Room C): *A small, shiny silver metal ring that fits into the lock of Silo C's door.*
4.  **Silver Key** (Room D): *A small, shiny silver metal ring that fits into the lock of Silo D's door.*
5.  **Lost Ship** (Room D): *The actual vessel you must find. It floats on the floor in the center of the room and has three hidden pockets.*

### **Puzzles (Total: 3)**
1.  **Riddle:** "A man walks into a room, sees nothing but walls, and says he is going to sleep. Who is this? A) The Captain B) The Ship C) A Clock D) The Silo"
2.  **Riddle:** "If I have one apple, two apples, three apples... how many apples do you have if the last one is missing? A) Four B) Three C) Two D) One"
3.  **Riddle:** "I am a river and I flow from sea to land. I am a ship and I can turn my sails. What am I?"

### **Clues (Total: 3)**
1.  **Description:** *A rusted key is found on the floor near Silo A's base door.*
2.  **Description:** *A clock runs for centuries, but its hands are broken and point to a time that has never happened.*
3.  **Description:** *A large wooden crate labeled "Pirate's Last" is found in the main room.*

## 5. Hint System

**How it works:**
*   **Hidden Items:** When you find an item, it is placed inside the Lost Ship (Room D). It acts as a clue for subsequent rooms.
    *   *Example:* If you find **Rusty Key** in Silo A, it helps you open Silo A's door and move to Room B.
    *   *Example:* If you find **Silver Key** in Silo B, it helps you open Silo B's door and move to Room C.
    *   *Example:* If you find **Silver Key** in Silo C, it helps you open Silo C's door and move to Room D.
*   **Clue System:** The clues (Riddles) are given as items when you find a clue. You must answer the riddle correctly to unlock a room. Each correct answer gives you one hint.

---

## 6. Game Flow: From Start to Finish

**Phase 1: The Silo Base (Room A)**
*   **Action:** Enter Room A with your **Rusty Key**.
*   **Interaction:** Open Silo A's door. You see a small wooden crate labeled "Pirate's Last." You find the key inside.
*   **Next Step:** Move to Room B with the key.
*   **Interaction:** Open Silo B's door. You see a small wooden crate labeled "Pirate's Last." You find the silver key inside.
*   **Next Step:** Move to Room C with the key.
*   **Interaction:** Open Silo C's door. You see a small wooden crate labeled "Pirate's Last." You find the silver key inside.
*   **Result:** You are now in Room D, ready for the final room.

**Phase 2: The Final Room (Room D)**
*   **Action:** Enter Room D with your last key.
*   **Interaction:** Walk into the large wooden crate labeled "Pirate's Last."
*   **Interaction:** Open the door to the massive Lost Ship. It floats on the floor.
*   **Interaction:** Go inside. You see three hidden pockets.
    *   Pocket 1: Contains **Rusty Key** (Hint for Silo A).
    *   Pocket 2: Contains **Silver Key** (Hint for Silo B).
    *   Pocket 3: Contains **Silver Key** (Hint for Silo C).
*   **Interaction:** Collect all three items to open the door of the Lost Ship.
*   **Result:** You can walk through the final room and exit safely to Captain Thorne's office.
